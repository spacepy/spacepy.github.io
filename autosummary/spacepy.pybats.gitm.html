spacepy.pybats.gitm¶

PyBats submodule for handling input/output for the Global Ionosphere-Thermosphere Model (GITM), one of the choices for the UA module in the SWMF.

Classes

GitmBin(filename, *args, **kwargs)

Object to open, manipulate and visualize 1 and 3 dimensional GITM output stored in binary format.
